1

🔌 Install Claude in Chrome

The extension that lets Claude fill out the form for you

▾
  1. Open Chrome and go to the Chrome Web Store:

    ↗ Install Claude for Chrome
  2. Click Add to Chrome and confirm.
  3. Click the Claude icon in your toolbar and sign in with your Spigen account (@spigen.com).
⚠️ Sign in with your Spigen account, not your personal Gmail. Claude needs access to your Spigen calendar and email to work correctly.
2

📅 Get Your Time-Off Calendar URL from UKG

This tells Claude which days you're off so it skips those days automatically

▾
  1. Log into UKG  ↗ Open UKG  and click My Time in the left sidebar.
  2. Under Time Off, click Calendar.
  3. Click the Sync Calendar button in the top right corner.
UKG: My Time → Time Off → Calendar → click "Sync Calendar" (top right)
  1. A popup appears — click Copy to copy the calendar URL.
Click Copy — you'll paste this URL in the next step
💡 Keep this URL handy — you'll paste it into the skill config in Step 3.

✎ Stored locally in this browser — just for your convenience

5

📨 SMS Reminder Setup

Texts your phone on Fridays if lunch wasn't submitted — even if your computer is off

▾

Two quick scripts work together. Part A runs on your Spigen account and checks if lunch was submitted. Part B runs on your personal Gmail and relays the alert to your phone as a text.

This auto-fills into the Part A prompt below ↓

💡 What is Google Apps Script? It's a free tool built into your Google account — no installation needed. You just open a webpage, paste some code, and Google runs it automatically on a schedule.

Part A — Spigen Google account (@spigen.com)

  1. Go to ↗ script.google.com (signed in as your Spigen account) → + New project → name it "Lunch Automation".
  2. Open Claude (chat) and send this message — Claude will paste the code, save it, and set the trigger automatically:
Go to my Apps Script Lunch Automation project, paste in the checkAndRemind code from the SKILL.md file, replace YOUR_PERSONAL_EMAIL with [email protected], save it, and set a weekly trigger every Friday 11am–12pm.

Part B — Personal Gmail account

⚠️ Make sure you're signed into your personal Gmail for this part — not your Spigen account. You may need to open an incognito window or switch accounts in the top-right corner of Google.
  1. Open a new tab, go to ↗ script.google.com signed in as your personal Gmail → + New project → name it "ForwardLunchReminder".
  2. Open Claude (chat) and send this message:
Go to my Apps Script ForwardLunchReminder project, paste in the forwardLunchReminder code from the SKILL.md file, replace PHONE_NUMBER with YOURNUMBER and CARRIER_GATEWAY with YOURGATEWAY, save it, and set a trigger every 10 minutes.
💡 Why two scripts? Carriers like Verizon block texts sent from corporate Google Workspace email. Your personal Gmail can send them just fine — so we relay through it.
